About Us

What Is EduAccessible?

EduAccessible is an education-based tech startup founded on one basic principle:

Accessibility is not an Afterthought.

More specifically, EduAccessible is committed to ensuring that no-one is unable to get a quality education due to their economic class and/or learning disability.

Through a combination of free and paid apps, our goal is to provide you with affordable solutions in a sustainable way.
